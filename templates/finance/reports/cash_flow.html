Understanding Cash Flow

• Cash Inflows - Money received from bookings, services, and other income sources

• Cash Outflows - Payments for expenses, salaries, supplies, and other costs

• Net Cash Flow - The difference between inflows and outflows (positive is good)

• Cash Flow Ratio - Measures ability to pay obligations (should be > 1.0)

Regular positive cash flow ensures business sustainability and growth
