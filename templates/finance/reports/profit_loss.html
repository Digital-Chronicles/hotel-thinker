Understanding Your P&L Statement

  • • Revenue - All income from bookings, services, and other sources
  • • Expenses - Operating costs including payroll, utilities, supplies, and maintenance
  • • Net Profit - Revenue minus expenses; positive numbers indicate profitability
  • • Profit Margin - Percentage of revenue that becomes profit (higher is better)
  • • Expense Ratio - Percentage of revenue spent on expenses (lower is better)
