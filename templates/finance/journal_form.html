Journal Entry Tips

After creating the journal entry, you'll be redirected to add journal lines (debits and credits).

A journal entry must have at least one debit and one credit line.

Total debits must equal total credits before posting.

Draft entries can be edited and deleted. Posted entries are permanently recorded.
